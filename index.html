guests

the guest list is intentionally mysterious and features a cast of characters from across the cleveland area and beyond. be prepared to talk to strangers and make friends.
attire is recommended.
we will have appetizers and cocktails (hill juices) available but would appreciate guests bringing beverages as well.

presentation details

we ask that all guests come prepared with a powerpoint, diorama, brochure, trifold poster, or other visual medium to explain their hill and why they will die on it.
if you would like to reserve a spot to give a 3-minute presentation to the whole class, please email hilldeath69@gmail.com with the topic and your visual medium.
while we always encourage debate, we ask kindly that each presenter be given time to speak without interruption.
also, this is a hill you would die on party, not a common opinion party, so try to make your hill something you believe in that is unique and you feel passionately about.
